## Horror

### Visual Approach
- Atmospheric over explicit gore
- Shadows and negative space
- Isolated elements (hand, eye, weapon)
- Gothic architecture
- Limited, moody color palettes

### Color Palettes
- **Classic**: Black, blood red, cream
- **Gothic**: Burgundy, gold, aged paper
- **Psychological**: Teal, rust, off-white
- **Supernatural**: Purple, green, black

### Template
```
[Horror element] in Mondo horror poster style, [isolated/atmospheric composition],
Gothic [architectural or natural element], [2-3 color palette], vintage [decade]
horror aesthetic, screen print with halftone shadows, [mood: dread/suspense/terror],
minimalist symbolic approach, no gore shown, suggestion over explicit imagery
```

### Examples

**Slasher:**
```
Single bloody knife silhouette against suburban house window in Mondo horror poster
style, centered minimalist composition, 3-color screen print: black, blood red,
moonlight blue, vintage 1978 slasher aesthetic, halftone shadow texture, isolated
weapon as symbol of unseen threat, suburban nightmare mood
```

**Gothic Horror:**
```
Victorian mansion with single candlelit window in Mondo horror poster style,
Gothic arched composition, twisted bare tree silhouette in foreground, 4-color
print: black, burgundy, cream, gold, Art Deco decorative border, vintage 1931
Universal Horror aesthetic, atmospheric dread, elegant decay
```

**Psychological Horror:**
```
Fragmented mirror reflecting distorted eye in Mondo horror poster style, geometric
shattered composition, 2-color print: rust orange and teal, negative space
creating unsettling gaps, vintage 1970s psychological thriller aesthetic, visual
representation of broken psyche, subtle disturbing mood
```

## Science Fiction

### Visual Approach
- Geometric and technical precision
- Vast scale contrasts (tiny human, huge ship)
- Symbolic technology (helmet, ship, planet)
- Retro-futurism
- Cool color palettes with accent colors

### Color Palettes
- **Space Opera**: Navy, silver, red accent
- **Cyberpunk**: Neon pink, electric blue, black
- **Alien Contact**: Teal, orange, cream
- **Dystopian**: Gray, rust, toxic yellow

### Template
```
[Sci-fi icon: helmet/ship/planet] in Mondo science fiction poster style,
[geometric/technical composition], [retro decade] futurism aesthetic, [cool
colors with accent], screen print with precise line work, [vast scale or
isolation theme], vintage [60s/70s/80s] sci-fi poster, sense of [wonder/danger]
```

### Examples

**Space Exploration:**
```
Astronaut helmet reflecting alien twin suns in Mondo sci-fi poster style,
centered symmetrical composition, duotone orange and deep blue, retro 1970s
futurism, screen print with precise technical details, sense of isolation and
discovery, vintage golden age sci-fi aesthetic, geometric planetary alignment
```

**Dystopian Future:**
```
Monolithic brutalist tower piercing smog layer in Mondo dystopian poster style,
geometric vertical composition, 3-color print: industrial gray, toxic yellow,
rust, Kilian Eng technical precision, oppressive architectural scale, vintage
1982 cyberpunk aesthetic, atmospheric pollution layers, humanity dwarfed by system
```

**Alien Encounter:**
```
UFO beam silhouette lifting human figure in Mondo sci-fi poster style, centered
vertical beam composition, 4-color print: navy, cream, neon green, silver,
minimalist symbolic abduction, vintage 1950s pulp sci-fi aesthetic, retro-futurism
meets screen print, sense of mysterious otherness
```

## Film Noir / Crime

### Visual Approach
- High contrast shadows
- Venetian blind shadows, rain effects
- Silhouettes and smoking guns
- Urban architecture
- Femme fatale silhouettes

### Color Palettes
- **Classic Noir**: Black, white, red accent
- **Neo-Noir**: Teal, magenta, black
- **Crime Thriller**: Navy, amber, cream

### Template
```
[Noir element: detective/gun/city] in Mondo film noir poster style, high contrast
[shadow technique], urban [skyline/alley] setting, [limited 2-3 color palette],
vintage [1940s/50s] crime poster aesthetic, screen print with dramatic shadows,
[mood: cynical/dangerous/mysterious], minimalist hardboiled style
```

### Examples

**Detective Noir:**
```
Detective silhouette in fedora and trench coat against rain-streaked window in
Mondo film noir poster style, venetian blind shadows across figure, city skyline
background, 3-color print: black, cream, red accent, vintage 1944 noir aesthetic,
high contrast dramatic lighting, screen print with halftone rain texture,
cynical urban atmosphere
```

**Heist Film:**
```
Smoking revolver with scattered poker chips in Mondo crime poster style,
minimalist centered composition, 2-color print: black and gold, Olly Moss
negative space approach, vintage 1950s heist aesthetic, symbolic objects tell
the story, tension in simplicity
```

- Southwestern color schemes
- Silhouettes at sunset

### Color Palettes
- **Classic Western**: Burnt orange, dusty brown, cream
- **Spaghetti Western**: Red, black, tan
- **Revisionist**: Muted earth tones

### Template
```
[Western icon: cowboy/landscape/weapon] in Mondo Western poster style, [vast
horizontal composition or lone silhouette], Southwestern [landscape element],
[warm earth tone palette], vintage [1960s/70s] Western poster aesthetic, screen
print with paper texture, [mood: epic/lonely/violent], minimalist frontier style
```

### Examples

**Classic Western:**

Lone cowboy silhouette on horseback against Monument Valley buttes in Mondo
Western poster style, horizontal epic landscape composition, 3-color print:

Extreme close-up of squinting eyes above bandana in Mondo Western poster style,

## Action / Thriller

### Visual Approach
- Dynamic angles and motion lines
- Explosive moments frozen
- Weapons and vehicles as focus
- Bold diagonal compositions
- High energy colors

### Color Palettes
- **80s Action**: Red, blue, yellow
- **Espionage**: Black, silver, red
- **Modern Thriller**: Orange, teal, black

### Template
```
[Action element: explosion/chase/weapon] in Mondo action poster style, [dynamic
diagonal or explosive composition], [bold contrasting colors], vintage [decade]
action poster aesthetic, screen print with motion blur effect, [high-octane mood],
geometric energy, minimalist maximum impact
```

### Examples

**80s Action:**
```
Muscle car mid-jump silhouette with explosion background in Mondo action poster
style, dynamic diagonal composition, 4-color print: red, electric blue, yellow,
black, vintage 1985 action blockbuster aesthetic, screen print with motion lines,
high-octane adrenaline, geometric explosion shapes, maximum impact minimal detail
```

**Spy Thriller:**
```
Sniper scope crosshairs over passport and gun in Mondo spy thriller poster style,
centered target composition, 3-color print: black, silver metallic, blood red,
Cold War era 1960s aesthetic, precise geometric circles, screen print with
halftone target pattern, tense espionage mood, symbolic objects over characters
```

## Genre Mixing

Combine genre templates when films blend styles:

```
[Film] in Mondo poster style, [Genre A] [visual element] meets [Genre B]
[visual element], [blended color palette], [combined mood]
```

**Example - Horror Comedy:**
```
Zombie hand holding ice cream cone in Mondo horror-comedy poster style, playful
horror juxtaposition, 3-color print: mint green, blood red, cream, vintage
1985 horror-comedy aesthetic, screen print with halftone shadows, absurd
gruesome humor, comedic monster imagery
```
